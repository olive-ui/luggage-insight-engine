## Key Insights

Customer sentiment across all brands is largely positive, showing that most products meet user expectations.

Safari and American Tourister have the highest positive sentiment, indicating better reliability and stronger customer trust.

Skybags and Nasher Miles perform well but show more variation in feedback, suggesting slightly inconsistent user experience.

VIP shows more mixed sentiment compared to other brands, indicating possible issues with consistency or durability.

The most common complaints are related to wheels, handles, and zippers, highlighting durability as the main concern.

Customers frequently mention value for money, lightweight design, and storage capacity as key positive factors.

Overall, while most brands perform well, improving durability would significantly increase customer satisfaction.
